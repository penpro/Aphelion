# Aphelion — Marketing Copy

> Product: **Aphelion** · Maker: **Penumbra** · Platform: **Windows** · Motif: corona eclipse + aphelion orbit

*Aphelion (n.) — the point in an orbit farthest from the sun. Your AI, at the farthest point from the cloud.*

---

## 1. Positioning statement (1 line)

**Aphelion is the all-in-one desktop app that runs powerful AI models entirely on your own Windows PC — no servers, no cloud, no setup.**

---

## 2. Elevator pitch (50 words)

Aphelion puts a full AI workspace on your own machine. Chat, write stories, build characters, branch dialogue, and code — all powered by local language models that never touch the cloud. No servers to wire up, no plumbing on top of other tools. It auto-fits the best model to your GPU and just runs.

---

## 3. Benefit-first feature bullets

- **Stays on your machine.** Every prompt, model, and conversation runs and lives locally — your data never leaves your PC, online or off.
- **Zero setup, not a stack.** Install one app and start talking. No servers, no containers, no Ollama-plus-six-other-things to glue together.
- **Auto-fits your hardware.** Aphelion reads your GPU and VRAM and loads the best model that'll run fast — no config files, no guesswork.
- **A real workspace, not just a chatbox.** Characters, stories, dialogue trees, prompts, and a world builder live side by side in one window.
- **Total privacy by default.** No account, no telemetry, no phone-home. The lock icon means what it says.
- **Open and yours.** Open source, free to download, and built to be inspected, extended, and trusted.

---

## 4. Store / GitHub description (100 words)

Aphelion is a free, open-source desktop app for Windows that runs large language models entirely on your own hardware. Where other setups make you stitch together servers, runtimes, and front-ends, Aphelion is a single install: open it, and it auto-fits the best model to your GPU and VRAM, then gets out of the way. Chat, write fiction, design characters, branch dialogue trees, draft and analyze code — all in one window, all offline. Nothing is sent to the cloud, there's no account, and there's no telemetry. Your AI, your machine, your rules. Download and run in minutes.
